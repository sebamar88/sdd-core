# Enterprise Profile

## Use When

- The change is broad, security-sensitive, compliance-heavy, multi-team, or operationally risky.
- Architecture, rollout, security, and test strategy need explicit review.
- Multiple implementation stages are expected.

## Required Artifacts

- `proposal.md`
- `delta-spec.md`
- `design.md`
- `tasks.md`
- `verification.md`
- `critique.md`
- `archive.md`

## Additional Recommended Artifacts

- Research notes.
- Architecture decision records.
- Security review.
- Test strategy.
- Rollout or migration plan.
- Operational risk assessment.

## Hard Gates

- Critique is mandatory.
- Security and operational risks must be documented.
- Rollout or migration impact must be explicit when relevant.
- Archive is blocked by unresolved verification or critique findings.

## Completion Evidence

- Requirement-to-evidence matrix.
- Review and critique record.
- Risk disposition.
- Archive record.
