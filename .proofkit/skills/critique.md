# Critique Skill

## Purpose

Create or refine `critique.md`.

## Inputs

- all active change artifacts
- verification evidence
- constitution

## Outputs

- verdict
- findings
- required fixes
- waivers

## Completion

Critique is complete when blockers, risks, and evidence gaps have been identified or ruled out.
