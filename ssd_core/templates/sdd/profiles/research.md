# Research Profile

## Use When

- The goal is discovery, comparison, feasibility analysis, or decision support.
- No implementation is planned in the current change.
- The output should inform a future proposal, design, or adapter.

## Required Artifacts

- `proposal.md` or research brief.
- Research findings.
- Recommendation or decision record.

## Required Evidence

- Research question.
- Sources inspected.
- Findings.
- Tradeoffs.
- Unresolved questions.

## Hard Gates

- Do not present research as implementation proof.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and recommendations.
- Cite source artifacts, URLs, files, or commands when possible.

## Completion Evidence

- Answer to the research question.
- Recommendation with rationale.
- Remaining uncertainty.
